Sofia Moshkina

Saros

Two young girls find themselves trapped under the oppressive rule of the Temple, a totalitarian regime that feeds on fear and obedience. As the system works relentlessly to break their bond, the girls must cling to each other and resist the forces determined to erase their dignity. Their courage becomes a testament to a love strong enough to survive even the harshest attempts to destroy it.

Featured — Interactivity · 2026

Jade Ng

Echoes of Mending

Echoes of Mending is an interactive projection-mapped dollhouse exploring themes of self-understanding and self-acceptance. Participants interact with miniature furniture through touching, opening, repairing, and exploring. These physical actions act as symbolic gestures that gradually transform each room and reveal animated responses within the space. By combining miniature environments, hand-drawn animation, projection mapping, and tactile interaction, the project investigates how symbolic play can emerge through material engagement and personal interpretation.
