Student Name — supervised by Staff Name
MAGI Studio 3 Exegesis, RMIT University, Melbourne, 2026
This exegesis documents the development of TAMAC1DE, an interactive installation that critiques predatory UX design through the lens of adversarial design theory. Drawing on DiSalvo's framework of agonistic design, Schüll's empirical research on machine-zone addiction mechanics, and Hjorth and Richardson's ambient play theory, the work positions familiar digital gestures — the swipe, the pull-to-reveal, the card flip — as sites of critique rather than compliance.
